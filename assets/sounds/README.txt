AUDIO FILES PLACEHOLDER
=======================

Please add the following MP3 files to this directory:

1. focus_music.mp3 - Piano instrumental for focus sessions (25 minutes)
2. break_music.mp3 - Relaxing piano music for short breaks (5 minutes)
3. long_break_music.mp3 - Calming piano music for long breaks (15 minutes)

Recommended characteristics:
- Piano instrumental music
- Calm and non-distracting
- Loop-friendly (seamless repeat)
- Good audio quality (128kbps or higher)

You can find royalty-free piano music from:
- YouTube Audio Library
- Freesound.org
- Pixabay Music
- Incompetech.com

Make sure the files are named exactly as listed above for the app to work properly.
